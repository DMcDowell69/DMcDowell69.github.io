A bike shop for all ages

Our goal is to get any and all of you to get on a new ride and to go out in to nature and have fun!

We carry all kinds of bike from any style you like to any type you are comfortable with.

We carry 11 types of bikes for your selection.
  1. Road Bike
  2. Mountain Bike
  3. Touring Bike
  4. Folding Bike
  5. Track Bike / Fixed Gear Bike
  6. BMX Bike
  7. Recumbent Bike(aka. Reclining Bike)
  8. Cruiser Bike
  9. Hybrid Bike
  10. Cyclocross Bike
  11. Electric Bike

If you can't find what you like in our store then we will make one custom for you.

If you are looking for a lower price we have a end of year sale.
